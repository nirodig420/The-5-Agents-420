# How I 10x'd My Mornings (You Won't Believe #3!!!)

So look, I used to be a total mess in the mornings. I'd hit snooze like five times,
roll out of bed at 9, and basically the whole day was already gone. It was bad.

The thing that changed everything was when I started planning my day the night before.
I use Notion for this — every evening I dump tomorrow's three big tasks into a simple
board, and that's it. No fancy system, no 40 plugins. Just three tasks.

The second thing: I stopped checking my phone first thing. Honestly this one was the
hardest. The trick that worked for me was leaving the phone charging in the kitchen
overnight. Out of sight, out of mind.

Third — and this is the big one everyone misses — I drink a full glass of water before
coffee. Sounds dumb. Changed my life. Your body is dehydrated after 8 hours of sleep
and the water wakes you up way better than caffeine does in those first ten minutes.

That's pretty much it. Three small things, no expensive gear.
